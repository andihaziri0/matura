# Product vision

## What we are building

A focused web application that helps Kosovo 12th-grade students prepare for **Testi i Maturës**, the state high-school exit exam. Built and operated by **AkademiaAS**, a private course teaching Math and programming.

## Why this exists

Students preparing for Matura today rely on:

- PDF scans of past papers shared on Telegram or Facebook,
- Printed booklets sold at bookstores,
- YouTube channels of varying quality,
- Private "kurse përgatitore" (in-person prep courses).

There is no widely-used digital tool that:

- Gives them **structured practice** in Albanian on every device,
- Tells them **how ready they actually are**, with calibrated feedback,
- Explains **why** they got something wrong, in their language,
- Recommends **what to study next** in a 20-minute block.

That is the wedge. We are not competing with another app — we are competing with a PDF on Telegram.

## Who it is for

- **Primary**: 12th-grade students in Kosovo, age 17–18, studying for Matura. Mobile-first, on inconsistent data, anxious and time-pressed.
- **Secondary (later)**: their teachers and tutors, who want to assign and review.
- **Operator**: AkademiaAS (the teacher and three students who own the system).

See [`personas.md`](personas.md) for fuller portraits.

## What success looks like

In order of importance:

1. A student opens the app, gets a clear "what to do next" prompt, and finishes a 10-question practice session in under 15 minutes.
2. The student understands *why* they got a question wrong because the explanation is in clear Albanian, with worked steps.
3. After a few sessions, the student gets a calibrated readiness signal per topic ("you are weak on funksione, strong on probabilitet").
4. The four owners (AkademiaAS staff) can author and publish a question in under 2 minutes through the admin UI.

We are not optimising for total content volume. We are optimising for **momentum** — the student returning tomorrow.

## What this is not

- **Not a curriculum.** We don't replace school. We supplement the run-up to Matura.
- **Not gamified.** Our users are 18, not 12. We celebrate streaks, never punish failure.
- **Not multi-country at MVP.** Kosovo Matura specifically. Albania's Maturë Shtetërore is similar but different — possible expansion, not a launch concern.
- **Not free of all friction.** We will require sign-up to track progress (after a 5-question try-without-account taste).

## The wedge

> A student in Pristina at 21:30 on a Tuesday opens the app on their phone, the home screen shows one button — "Vazhdo me Funksionet, 12 minuta" — they tap it, do 10 questions, see a clean explanation in Albanian for the two they got wrong, see their topic score tick up, and close the app.

If we nail that loop, the rest follows.
